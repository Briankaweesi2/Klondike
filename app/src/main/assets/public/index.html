Privacy

Klondike runs the solitaire game locally on your device. The game does not require an account and does not ask you to enter personal information.

This app uses Google AdMob to show banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads. Google and its partners may collect device identifiers, app activity, diagnostics, approximate location, and ad interaction data to provide, limit, measure, and improve ads.

Rewarded ads are optional and only run after you choose to watch one for extra hints or to unlock the Reserve slot for the current deal. Interstitial ads are limited to natural game breaks.
