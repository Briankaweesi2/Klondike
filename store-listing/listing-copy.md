## Full description (max 4000 characters)

Solitaire Coach: Klondike is a clean, classic game of Klondike solitaire with a built-in coach to help you play smarter.

Deal the cards across the traditional 7-column tableau, draw from the stock, and build all four suit foundations from Ace to King. Every game tracks your time, move count, and score, and a victory screen sums up your result when you clear the board.

Stuck on a move? The Hint button highlights a good next play — you get 3 free hints in every deal, and you can optionally watch a short video ad for 3 more. Prefer a nudge instead of a clue? The AI Move button plays a suggested move for you. And if you change your mind, Undo has you covered.

FEATURES

- Classic Klondike solitaire: 7-column tableau, draw pile, and 4 suit foundations
- Hint system: 3 free hints per deal that highlight a good next move
- AI Move button that plays a suggested move for you
- Undo your moves
- Timer, move counter, and score tracking on every deal
- Victory screen with your final time, moves, and score
- Two table themes: a green-felt Classic look and a dark Sapphire (navy and silver) look
- Optional Reserve slot: unlock an extra card slot for the current deal by watching a rewarded ad
- Fully playable offline
- No account or sign-in required — just open the app and play

Solitaire Coach is a free card game. There is no real-money gambling of any kind: no wagering, no cash prizes, and nothing to buy — the casino-style felt is purely a visual theme.

A note on ads: this app is free and supported by advertising (banner and occasional interstitial ads at natural breaks between games). Rewarded video ads are always optional and only play when you choose to watch one for extra hints or to unlock the Reserve slot. You can review your ad and privacy choices any time from the in-app Privacy screen.
