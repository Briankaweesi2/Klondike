Privacy Policy

Effective July 17, 2026 · Published by EdgeHog · com.vimbah.klondike

The short version: the game runs entirely on your device, we never ask you for personal information, and the only data collection happening is performed by Google's advertising services — which is what keeps the app free.

1 — Information we do not collect

Solitaire Coach: Klondike has no account and no sign-in of any kind. We never ask for your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or any other personal information — there are no forms or fields to enter them.

We, the developer, do not operate any server that receives your data. Your game state — the cards, timer, moves, and score — is processed locally on your device. The game is fully playable offline.

2 — Information collected by Google advertising services

The app is free and supported by ads. It uses Google AdMob to show:

  • Banner ads, displayed during play
  • Interstitial ads, limited to natural breaks between games
  • Rewarded video ads, always optional — shown only when you choose to watch one, for 3 extra hints or to unlock the Reserve card slot for the current deal

To provide, limit, personalize, measure, and protect these ads — including analytics and anti-fraud checks — Google and its advertising partners may collect data from your device, including:

  • Device identifiers, such as your device's advertising ID
  • App activity and ad interaction data — for example, which ads were shown, tapped, or watched
  • Diagnostics, crash, and performance data
  • Approximate (coarse) location, inferred for ad delivery where permitted
  • Device, network, and browser/WebView information

This data is collected by Google's SDKs on the app's behalf and handled under Google's own policies:

  • How Google uses data from partner apps
  • Google Privacy Policy

3 — Consent and your choices

The app integrates Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP). Where required — for example in the European Economic Area and the UK — you'll see a consent form before personalized ads are served, and you can decline personalized advertising.

You can revisit your ad privacy choices any time from Ad Choices on the app's in-app Privacy screen, or limit/reset ad tracking in your device's own privacy settings.

Because the app has no account and stores nothing about you on any server we control, uninstalling the app stops all data collection associated with it.

4 — Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, and we do not knowingly serve targeted advertising to them. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app and that data may have been collected through the advertising services above, contact us below and we'll help, including directing the request to Google where applicable.

If this app's Play Store listing later targets children, this policy and the app's ad configuration will be updated to comply with children's privacy and advertising requirements before that change takes effect.

5 — Not a gambling product

Solitaire Coach: Klondike is a free classic card game. It offers no real-money gambling of any kind — no wagering, no cash or prize payouts, and no purchasing of chips or currency. The casino-style felt look is a visual theme only.

6 — Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and revise the effective date above. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.
